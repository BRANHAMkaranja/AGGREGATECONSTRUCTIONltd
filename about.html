Who We Are

Aggregate Construction Ltd was formed as a result of management re-structuring. Whilst in association with Twiga Construction Company, we have completed number of projects including hospital, shopping centers, oﬃce blocks and high-end residential units. We have very experienced staﬀ both in qualiﬁcations and in experience where most have worked well over 10 years in the industry. Our main goals are to deliver projects on budget and time, which is where speciality comes in . Our experience in the industry has been in variety of projects such as housing developments, oﬃce blocks, interior ﬁt-out works and major refurbishments of iconic buildings in various places in Kenya.However ,we are also keen on developing young talents by sourcing employment locally right from casual workers to construction engineers .
