Our Values

The Director, Management & Staﬀ of Aggregate Construction Ltd pledges that all company activities & decisions will be guided on the following core values:-
» Client satisfaction
» Protection of the environment
» Health & Safety for all workers
» Quality Service
» Commitment to professionalism
» Integrity – Fairness & Honesty
» Development of Human Resource
» Untiring eﬀort for improvement
» Courtesy & Humility
» Co-operation & Team Spirit

Aggregate Construction Limited is committed to ensuring the health, Safety and welfare of its employees, so far as is reasonably practicable. The company also fully accepts responsibility for other persons who may be aﬀected by its activities. The management will ensure that all processes and systems of work are designed to take account of health and safety and are properly supervised by competent persons at all times.

Aggregate Construction Ltd provides a full range of services to its clients, starting at the needs assessment phase, through the project identiﬁcation and justiﬁcation phases, to the design of the project, its implementation through procurement and construction, project management, maintenance, operation and management.We strive to execute our projects in such a way as to contribute towards capacity building in the communities where we operate. Employment creation, transfer of knowledge and skills to local communities is part of our main objectives in our community involvement in projects.We have a tradition of assisting our clients at management level and providing support to enhance their institutional capacity through training.

We strive to be the industry's partner of choice, by delivering quality and value in the various construction sectors through integrated solutions and services.Aggregate Construction Ltd strives always to create a desirable place of work within a safe working environment.
